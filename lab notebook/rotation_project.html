Notes:

    Ways to identify outliers:

    • IQR: data range is determined by Q1-1.5*IQR(Q3-Q1) and Q3+1.5*IQR; oftentimes, interested to limit the specs at 3 std therefore, should be 1.7 instead of 1.5
    • Standard deviation: upper limit=mean+3*std, lower limit=mean-3*std
    • Z-score: convert the data into another dataset with mean=0; usually use 3 as a cut-off
